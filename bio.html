My Degree

I studied at Trinity College, Cambridge from October 2016 to July 2019.

There, I completed the Natural Sciences Tripos, aiming towards biological modules. Details of these can be found further down on this page.

I really enjoyed my time there; though the work was intense and deadlines were constantly looming, I persevered through and made some great friends along the way.

Year 1: Biology of Cells, Physiology of Organisms, Chemistry, Mathematical Biology

Year 2: Biochemistry and Molecular Biology, Pharmacology, Biology of Disease

Year 3: Neuroscience

I have gained experience of working in the lab, including how to follow many procedures, health and safety and coordinating with others.

I attended computer labs, where I gained basic programming skills and learned how to display data effectively.

I've produced several presentations, both alone and in groups, as well as a scientific poster.

I honed my writing skills through essays and my final dissertation, titled "A Critique on Cognitive Enhancement".
