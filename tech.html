WEBSITE REVIEW

Overall I am happy with the outcome of this website. I believe it has its strengths, but there are still a few weaknesses. The strengths include that the backgrounds make it quite pretty to look at (though I may be biased because they are my own photos), there is a variety of content to read about, and it should be easy to use and navigate. The weaknesses include that the interface is pretty basic. While minimalism can be popular, I think this should be backed up by having interesting features such as animations when you hover over things. I wish I had more time so I could get the site looking exactly how I want it to, but I believe I have done well with the time I had.
